Indomie Recipe

INDOMIE!!!

The BEST instant noodle in the world.

Once you taste this instant noodle, there will be no other instant noodle that can compete with this.

Ingredients

  • 2 Packs of Indomie Noodles
  • 2 glasses of water
  • 3 small red pepper
  • 1 small ball of Onions
  • Preferred meat source (Eggs or Chicken are the most widely used).
  • 3 Sausages
  • Green beans and carrot

Steps:

  1. Pour 2 glasses of water into a clean pot and leave to simmer on medium flame.
  2. Add spices (in the Ramen packet), add mixed vegetables with chopped pepper and onions and allow to boil for 1 minute. Make sure the Pot is covered from the beginning.
  3. Now break the Noodles and pour into the boiling water with all those ingredients already in. Distribute it evenly around the pot then cover. Leave to cook for 5 minutes while occasionally stirring it so the heat goes around.
  4. Just before it is done, add the Sausages (you want them semi cooked so they can retain a little grit).
