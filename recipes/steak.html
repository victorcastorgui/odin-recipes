Steak Recipe

Perfect Air Fry Steak

Who doesn't love steak? Steak are a necessity in life.

In this page, you will see the easiest way to cook a steak using Air Fryer. It is simple and juicy without any hassle of cleaning up your kitchen top.

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 4 sirloin steaks, 1 inch thick (1 1/2 lbs total)
  • olive oil spray

Instructions:

  1. Combine the spices in a small bowl.
  2. Spray the steak with olive oil and coat both sides with the spices.
  3. Preheat the air fryer so the basket gets hot. For a 1-inch steak, air fry 400F 10 minutes turning halfway, for medium rare, for medium, cook 12 minutes, flipping halfway. See temp chart below, time may vary slightly with different air fryer models, and the thickness of the steaks.
  4. Finish with a pinch of more salt and black pepper.
  5. Let it rest, tented with foil 5 minutes before slicing.
